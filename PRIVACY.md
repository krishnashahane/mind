# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Overview

Mind runs entirely in your browser. We do not operate servers that process, store, or transmit your data.

## What stays on your device

- All AI model inference
- Your chat history
- Uploaded documents and file embeddings
- Voice input processing
- Custom memory and preferences
- Exported chat files

## What we do not do

- Collect personal information
- Send data to external servers
- Use cookies or tracking pixels
- Store anything outside your browser's local storage
- Share data with third parties

## Offline usage

After downloading a model, Mind works without an internet connection. Your data never leaves your machine.

## Your control

You can clear all stored data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for the application.

## Security

Data security depends on your device and browser security settings. We recommend keeping your browser up to date.

## Contact

Questions? Open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/krishnashahane/mind/issues).
